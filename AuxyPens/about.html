About AuxyPens

Since 1950, AuxyPens has been a leading name in crafting premium writing instruments. From our humble beginnings in the USA, we have grown into a brand known for its quality and innovation. Founded by Edward Bradley, Lillian Harper, and Jonathan Cole, AuxyPens continues to deliver excellence in every pen we create.

Edward Bradley

The visionary behind AuxyPens, Edward’s passion for design and quality led to the creation of our first premium fountain pen.

Lillian Harper

As the creative force of the team, Lillian brought modern aesthetics and innovation to our pen designs.

Jonathan Cole

Jonathan’s focus on craftsmanship and precision has ensured that every AuxyPen delivers the perfect writing experience.
